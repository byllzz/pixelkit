The ultimate workflow for SVG icons

PixelKit is a lightweight, client-first icon browser designed to bridge the gap between design and code. Instead of digging through folders, you can search, preview, and customize your entire library in one sleek interface.

Every icon is a first-class citizen. Recolor on the fly, resize without losing quality, and copy optimized SVG code to your clipboard instantly. Our engine ensures that every export is minified and ready for production.

Get started for free by running npm install @pixelkit/core. Whether you use React, Vue, or vanilla HTML, PixelKit fits perfectly into your tech stack. Unlock the Pro version for advanced team collaboration and custom icon hosting.

What is PixelKit?
PixelKit is a lightweight, client-first SVG icon browser: search, resize, recolor, and copy pixel-perfect SVGs instantly.
How do I use it?
Follow our quick-start guide to integrate the icon browser into your workflow.
Can I recolor and resize icons?
Yes, the built-in editor allows for real-time SVG manipulation.
What formats are available?
We primarily support SVG, but you can also export to PNG and PDF.
Is PixelKit free to use?
We offer a robust free tier and a pro version for advanced features.
Can I use icons in commercial projects?
Yes, our license covers both personal and commercial use.
What happens if the CDN fails?
Our local fallback system ensures your icons remain visible.
